About Me

Hi, I'm Gochi — a passionate painter from the Philippines.

My work explores memory, nature, and human emotion through rich textures and vibrant colors. I primarily work with acrylic and oil on canvas, creating bold, layered pieces inspired by Philippine landscapes and everyday life.

Open for commissions & collaborations.
